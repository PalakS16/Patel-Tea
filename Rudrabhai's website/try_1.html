About Patel Tea

Established in 1961, Patel Tea has been a symbol of excellence in the tea industry, offering a rich and authentic tea experience for over six decades. Our journey began with a commitment to bring the finest tea leaves from the lush gardens of Assam to the homes of tea lovers. Since then, we have grown to become one of the top tea brands in Karnataka, known for our unwavering dedication to quality. All of our teas are sourced from certified suppliers, ensuring that every batch meets international quality standards. We take pride in processing the finest tea leaves using state-of-the-art methods, preserving their natural flavor and health benefits. Our strict adherence to quality has helped us carve a niche in the market, earning the trust and loyalty of tea connoisseurs across Karnataka.

From the fields of Assam to your cup, Patel Tea promises purity, freshness, and an exceptional tea-drinking experience. We are committed to continuing our legacy of excellence, offering only the best to our esteemed customers.
